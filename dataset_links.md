# Dataset Availability

The datasets used in this study are publicly available and listed below:

## Gesture Recognition Datasets
- **20BN Jester Dataset**  
  https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/toxicmender/20bn-jester  

- **EgoHands Dataset**  
  https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/himaniishah/egohands  

## Fall Detection Datasets
- **UR Fall Detection Dataset**  
  https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shahliza27/ur-fall-detection-dataset  

- **Multiple Cameras Fall Dataset**  
  https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/soumicksarker/multiple-cameras-fall-dataset  

## Fire Detection Datasets
- **Fire Dataset**  
  https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/phylake1337/fire-dataset  

- **BoWFire Dataset**  
  https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/malligasenthil/bowfire  

- **Forest Fire Image Classification Dataset**  
  https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/obulisainaren/forest-fire-c4  

---

## Notes
All datasets are publicly accessible and were used for research purposes.  
No proprietary or restricted datasets were used.

Preprocessing steps (frame extraction, resizing, normalization, and sequence generation) were applied as described in the methodology section of the paper.
